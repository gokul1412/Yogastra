Our Story

A sanctuary dedicated to your holistic well-being since 2015.

A Space for Transformation

Founded in 2015, Yogastra was born from a simple desire: to create an inclusive, calming space where people from all walks of life could come to find balance. Our founder, Maya Lin, envisioned a studio that went beyond physical fitness, focusing equally on mental clarity and spiritual growth.

Over the years, our small community has grown into a vibrant family. We've expanded our class offerings, but our core ethos remains the same: practice with intention, live with compassion.

Our Mission

To empower individuals on their wellness journey through expert guidance, mindful movement, and supportive community.

Our Vision

A world where physical and mental well-being is accessible to everyone, fostering global peace starting from within.

Our Values

Inclusivity, compassion, continuous learning, and respect for the ancient traditions of yoga.
